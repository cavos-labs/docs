## Usage-Based Pricing

Cavos has no subscriptions, no tiers, and no per-user fees. You only pay for the gas your users consume.

### How it works

1. **Deposit STRK** into your organization's Gas Tank via the [Billing Dashboard](https://cavos.xyz/dashboard/billing)
2. A **5% platform fee** is deducted from each deposit
3. The remaining balance sponsors your users' gasless transactions
4. Gas costs are deducted atomically with each sponsored transaction

### What's included

| Feature | Cost |
|---------|------|
| SDK access (React & React Native) | Free |
| Account creation & deployment | Sponsored from your Gas Tank |
| Gasless transactions (Paymaster) | Sponsored from your Gas Tank |
| Session keys & policies | Free |
| Dashboard & analytics | Free |
| Testnet (Sepolia) | **Completely free** — no deposit needed |

### Testnet vs Mainnet

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Sepolia (Testnet)">
    On Sepolia, Cavos operates a **free pool**. You do not need to deposit any tokens — all transactions are sponsored automatically.

    This is ideal for development and testing.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Mainnet">
    On Mainnet, you must deposit STRK into your Gas Tank before your users can execute transactions.

    Monitor your balance in the [Billing Dashboard](https://cavos.xyz/dashboard/billing) to prevent transaction failures.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Managing your Gas Tank

Go to **[cavos.xyz/dashboard/billing](https://cavos.xyz/dashboard/billing)** to:
- View your current STRK balance
- Deposit STRK from any Starknet wallet
- Track deposit history and gas consumption

<Info>
  If your users see an `"x-paymaster-api-key is invalid"` error, it almost always means your organization has run out of gas. Deposit more STRK to resolve this.
</Info>
